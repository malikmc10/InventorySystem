This project is outlining an inventory management system having the following features:
POS - when an item is sold the quantity is reduced in the store inventory, on screen POS machine
Warehouse - where products are sent from to the individual stores (invoice, order request)
Manager Side - gets a notice when store product quantity is 5 or less, allowed to make request for supplies, update the inventory when supplies arrive, upload video survillance 
contains validation and login
